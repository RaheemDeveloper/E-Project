Our Theatre Awards

Lux Style Icon of the Year (2022)

Fawad Khan

Fawad Khan started his career in theatre and music before becoming a film superstar. In 2022, he was honored as Icon of the Year at the Lux Style Awards for his contributions to Pakistan's performing arts scene.

Lux Style Award – Best Actress (2023)

Saba Qamar

Saba Qamar started her career with theatre and later became one of Pakistan’s top TV and film actresses. She won the Lux Style Award 2023 for her film "Kamli".

Pakistan Excellence Award – Lifetime Achievement (2023)

Sania Saeed

Sania Saeed is one of Pakistan’s most respected actors with decades of work in theatre, television, and film. In 2023, she received the Pakistan Excellence Award for Lifetime Achievement.
